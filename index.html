Our Story

At RJ Mexican Cuisine, we blend traditional Mexican techniques with bold Tex-Mex flavors to define the Dallas dining experience. Our mission is to engage all your senses through imaginative comfort food and award-winning cocktails in a cozy, unpretentious setting. From our signature Tilapia Margarita to our famous fajitas, every dish we serve is crafted with a unique twist designed to make every bite unforgettable.
